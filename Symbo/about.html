ABOUT THE AAACET

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering has well-established Computer Laboratories with state-of-art infrastructure to cater the needs of the Engineering students. All computer laboratories are fully air-conditioned and the students can access the laboratories round the clock. Highly sophisticated and the latest configuration computers were installed in various laboratories to cater to the students quest for knowledge.

All the computer laboratories are equipped with structured Wi-Fi, LAN (CAT6 Cable), Fibre Cable and other relevant software. Computer laboratories are equipped with 323 computers (DELL, N-Computing, Thin_Client), 8 servers, 22 printers and over 11 laptops (DELL) with a dedicated network. The Computer laboratories are being upgraded from time to time to provide the best computational infrastructure.
