CSE TECHFUSION-2K24

The CSE SYMPOSIUM “TechFusion 2K24” – 28.10.2024 & 29.10.2024 Department of Computer Science and Engineering in association with CSI Student Chapter organizes a National Level Technical Symposium “TechFusion 2K24” on 28th & 29th October, 2024. For Paper Presentation send your abstract to: techfusion2k24@aaacet.ac.in Symposium is an annual flagship event organized by the Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) department, aimed at bringing together students, professionals, and academicians to explore the latest advancements in technology. This symposium serves as a platform for sharing knowledge, fostering innovation, and networking within the tech community. It features a variety of activities, including technical workshops, paper presentations, coding competitions, hackathons, and expert talks from industry leaders. Participants not only gain hands-on experience but also get exposure to cutting-edge research and trends in areas such as AI, machine learning, blockchain, and cybersecurity. Zenith is an excellent opportunity for aspiring engineers to challenge themselves, collaborate with peers, and contribute to the ever-evolving tech landscape.
